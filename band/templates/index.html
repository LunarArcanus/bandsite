In 2008 we started off as a small group of musicians. We knew where we were going, but we didn't know how to get there. Local performances was something we had already experienced - and experienced again - and after being signed up by a label, we released 2 albums. Both sold out, but as soon as the contract was over, we were let off.

The label went bankrupt a short while later.

It was then we realised that such one-sided relationships were always short-lived, and what we needed was a worthwhile support that could both fund us and share our 'treasures'. There had to be a fragment of genuine care from both parties.
Eventually tiring of the search, we decided that it was time to start one up ourselves. And well, here we are.

Our goal is to promote artists and expose them to the crowd that may otherwise never hear of them. Slowly but surely, we have been gaining notable affiliates to join our cause and now we stand strong with some of the most universally-acclaimed agencies backing us.
So what are you waiting for? Take that stage!
